Wine of Zamorak

Description

Wines of Zamorak cannot be consumed, but are used as a secondary ingredient in creating ranging and bastion potions. The bloody grimoire states that the wine is incredibly unholy but has a sweet taste that makes it easily addictive. It is possible that the wine is used in the making of bloody bracers, as they have a similar side effect upon drinking.

Ingredients

  • Zamorak's grapes
  • Jug of water

Steps

  1. Mash grapes
  2. Mix mashed grapes with jug of water
