Botanical Pie

Description

A delicious pie that increases your skill with herbalism allowing you to craft better potions! It's made with delicious plant known as the Golovanova.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Pie Dish
  • Golovanova fruit top

Steps

  1. Mix water and flour to form pastry dough
  2. Form pastry dough to fill pie dish
  3. Add Golovanova fruit top to pie shell
  4. Cook on nearest cooking range or fire
  5. Enjoy and craft more powerful potions!
