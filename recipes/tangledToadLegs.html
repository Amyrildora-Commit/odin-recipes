Tangled Toad Legs

Description

A breadbowl filled with frog legs and a mix of other ingredients which create a delicious gnomish treat.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Breadbowl Mould
  • 4 Frog Legs
  • 2 Cheese chunks
  • 2 Equa Leaves
  • 1 Gnome Spice
  • 1 Dwellberry

Steps

  1. Fill a breadbowl mould with dough and bake the breadbowl halfway
  2. Take the half baked breadbowl and mix in 4 toad legs, 2 cheese chunks, 2 Equa leaves, 1 Gnome spice, and 1 Dwellberry
  3. Cook the breadbowl filled with soup and frog legs until the breadbowl is golden brown
